THE 1st International Conference on Emerging Trends in Information Technology and Engineering - ICONITE


Nowadays, we are still in a prolonged pandemic periode. Demands creativity that never goes out. Must be creative to fill science in life. Among the sciences are Design, Management, Information Technology which is very supportive in our life.

The 3rd International Conference on Information Technology, Multimedia, Architecture, Design and E-Business (IMADE) is the third international conference. At 2023, The 3rd International Conference IMADE organized by INSTIKI with some famous university such as Udayana University, IDB Bali and supported by Bina Nusantara University. In this conference, International Conference IMADE would like to accommodate various ideas from academics, participants, officials, creative industries performer, and policymakers from all over the world to contribute to the development of science and creative industries both in Indonesia to the global level. As well as being creative in the pandemic era, realizing togetherness and collaboration from various scientific fields. Authors are invited to contribute and submit papers covering any of the subtopics.

Obtained

Aims and Purposes

  • To publish researchers findings and service providers in the recognizable international indexed proceedings.
  • To collect national and international solution and findings in design, business, and scientific forums.
  • To expand professional networks within area of design, business, and scientific information.
  • To elaborate, share, and contribute in knowledge and dicussion in international indexed forum.
  • To aim and focus on post-pandemic economy wellfare issue regarding creative and technological usage.

Benefits

  • As a contribution for the stakeholders, and policymakers in terms of design, business, data and findings; which are expected to be used as references and input for sustainable Indonesian development, especially in the technology, management, design and business.
  • Increasing collaboration between researchers, professionals, and industries from various universities or institution in both national and international.
  • Improving the number of high-quality indexed publications from academia value point of view.
